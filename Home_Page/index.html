About Winter Storm

Winter Storm is a community-driven crypto project on the Base chain, featuring a deflationary token model with a 6% transaction tax. This tax redistributes 3% to token holders and fortifies the liquidity pool with another 1% for stable trading. The remaining 2% is used to buy back $STM tokens which is sent to a dead wallet, contributing to token burning and scarcity. Winter Storm prioritizes community engagement through regular events. Leveraging the Arbitrum network, the project ensures scalability, cost-efficiency, and increased interoperability benefits.

For Holders

WINTER STORM incentivices holders of $STORM token through a unique reward system. This innovative mechanism allocates a 3% transaction tax from each buy and sell transaction to be redistributed among existing $STORM holders, in the form of Eth coin (Eth). To further enhance user experience, the STORM Holders Rewards Dashboard empowers $STORM token holders with real-time insights, allowing them to effortlessly track their accumulated rewards and current token balance, fostering a more engaged and informed community.

For Stakers

WINTER STORM motivates users to invest in and stake the $STORM token through a dynamic reward system. This remarkable feature dispenses rewards based on the quantity of $STORM tokens staked and the duration of stake period, rewarding participants with additional $STORM tokens ($STM). To enhance the staking experience, the Staking Dashboard enables $STORM stakers to initiate stakes, monitor accrued rewards, and check current $STORM token balance with ease and transparency.
